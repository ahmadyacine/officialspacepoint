Quick Flight

Give Students Their First Real Exposure to Space Technology

The Quick Flight Program is designed to introduce students to the exciting world of space technology through an engaging and hands-on CubeSat experience. In just a few hours, participants move beyond traditional classroom learning and step into the role of future aerospace innovators by exploring how real satellites are designed, built, and operated.

Students interact with core STEM concepts through practical activities that transform theory into action, making complex engineering ideas simple, exciting, and memorable. The program encourages curiosity, creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving while giving learners a meaningful first look into the rapidly growing space industry.

Quick Flight is the perfect introduction for schools looking to inspire students with a high-impact STEM experience that is both educational and unforgettable. Whether for large student groups, awareness events, or introductory workshops, the program creates excitement around science, engineering, and innovation in an accessible and engaging format.

Learning Outcomes

  • • Gain a clear introduction to space technology, satellite systems, and modern aerospace concepts
  • • Experience hands-on CubeSat assembly and interactive engineering activities
  • • Understand how science, technology, engineering, and mathematics connect to real-world applications
  • • Develop problem-solving, collaboration, and critical thinking skills through team-based challenges
  • • Build confidence in STEM learning through practical and engaging experiences
  • • Explore future academic and career pathways in engineering, technology, and the space sector
  • • Strengthen creativity, innovation, and communication skills in a dynamic learning environment
  • • Experience an inspiring introduction to aerospace designed for learners aged 12+
Duration: 2-3 Hours
Best For: First-time exposure & large student groups
Cost: 500 AED

Shallow Flight

Turn Curiosity Into Real Skills

The Shallow Flight Program is an immersive STEM experience designed for students ready to move beyond the basics and begin developing real engineering skills through hands-on satellite learning. Participants explore real satellite subsystems and aerospace engineering concepts while actively building, connecting, and testing components themselves.

By combining mechanical, electrical, and software learning into one integrated experience, students gain a deeper understanding of how modern space systems function. Rather than simply learning concepts theoretically, participants apply their knowledge through practical activities that strengthen technical thinking, collaboration, and innovation.

The program encourages students to think like engineers by introducing structured problem-solving, system integration, and mission-oriented teamwork in an engaging and supportive environment. Shallow Flight is ideal for schools aiming to develop practical STEM capabilities and provide students with a more advanced and meaningful aerospace learning journey.

Learning Outcomes

  • • Develop practical engineering and technical skills through hands-on satellite system activities
  • • Learn the fundamentals of mechanical, electrical, and software integration in aerospace applications
  • • Build, connect, test, and troubleshoot real satellite-related components and subsystems
  • • Strengthen analytical thinking and structured problem-solving abilities
  • • Experience collaborative project workflows similar to real engineering environments
  • • Improve teamwork, communication, and technical decision-making skills
  • • Gain deeper exposure to applied STEM learning beyond traditional classroom theory
  • • Begin thinking with an engineering mindset through mission-based learning experiences
  • • Build valuable future-ready skills relevant to schools, universities, and professional development programs
Duration: 6-10 Hours
Best For: Skill-building & deeper engagement
Cost: 3000 AED

The Mission

From Classroom to Space Mission

The Mission is SpacePoint’s most advanced and transformative aerospace learning experience, designed to simulate the complete journey of a real satellite mission from concept to execution. Students step into the role of aerospace engineers and mission specialists as they work through the same processes used in real-world space programs.

Participants experience the full satellite development cycle, including system design, assembly, integration, testing, mission simulation, and operational planning. The program also introduces launch concepts, environmental challenges faced in space, communication systems, and mission operations, giving students a realistic understanding of how space missions are executed.

Built for ambitious learners and flagship school initiatives, The Mission combines advanced technical learning with leadership, collaboration, and critical thinking development. The experience creates high-impact educational outcomes that schools can proudly showcase while giving students an unforgettable opportunity to engage with real aerospace engineering practices.

More than just a course, The Mission is a defining educational experience that transforms curiosity into innovation and inspires the next generation of space pioneers.

Learning Outcomes

  • • Experience the complete lifecycle of a satellite mission from concept to operations
  • • Gain advanced exposure to aerospace engineering, systems integration, and mission planning
  • • Build and test satellite systems while understanding real operational and environmental challenges
  • • Learn how real engineering teams collaborate, solve problems, and manage complex projects
  • • Strengthen leadership, communication, and high-level critical thinking skills
  • • Develop confidence in technical presentation, teamwork, and innovation-driven environments
  • • Apply engineering and scientific concepts in realistic mission simulation scenarios
  • • Create showcase-worthy projects and measurable STEM achievements for schools, universities, and organizations
  • • Gain future-focused skills aligned with the growing global space and technology industries
  • • Experience a transformative hands-on program designed to inspire the next generation of innovators and space leaders
Duration: 18-24 Hours
Best For: Advanced programs & flagship initiatives
Investment: ~7500 AED
